# Commercial Licensing

This page is for organizations evaluating private licensing, closed-source use, OEM embedding, or commercial support around YAi!.

YAi! Community is distributed under the repository open-source license.

Separate commercial licensing may be available for organizations that need proprietary embedding, closed-source redistribution, OEM use, private deployment, commercial support, or enterprise terms.

## When Commercial Terms May Apply

Commercial terms may be relevant when an organization wants to:

- embed YAi! in a closed-source product
- distribute a modified build without open-sourcing the modifications
- run private or internal deployments with non-standard procurement or support requirements
- obtain architecture review, private support, or custom integration work
- discuss enterprise or OEM-style usage that is not covered by the repository license

## Scope Note

Unless explicitly stated in this repository, YAi! Community remains the open-source core. Any private commercial or enterprise terms, support arrangements, or future enterprise modules are separate from the public repository materials.

Use [SUPPORT.md](SUPPORT.md) for support-path guidance and [LICENSE](LICENSE) plus [NOTICE.md](NOTICE.md) for repository licensing context.
